What is Bricksite?

Bricksite is a Content Management System (CMS), which is a system that allows users without web development experience, to create beautiful, interactive and response websites.

Our focus is an easily usable system, that gives everyone the chance to own a personalized website. Another big focus of ours, is our support, which is rewarded with incredibly good feedback from our customers.
